We're a group of people interested in exploring how we can interact with data to make positive change for local Delaware communities. We are civic-minded. We are developers. We are designers. We are beginners. We are experts. We are everything in-between. It doesn't matter how much experience you have, all you need is a willingness to learn and a desire to help your community. We'll meet regularly to work on projects and build community together.
